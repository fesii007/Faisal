The Shift

Pretty edits do not save weak creative.

Your ad has a brutal job: earn attention, explain the offer fast, make the product feel worth trying, and give the viewer a reason to take action now.

That is where I work. I take your product footage, UGC, testimonials, AI visuals, voiceover, or raw idea and turn it into ad creative that is structured for the sale.

1
Hooks that stop the right buyer
The first seconds are built around the viewer's pain, desire, objection, curiosity, or proof point — not random effects or flashy transitions that look good but say nothing.
2
Edits that make the offer clear
Fast pacing, captions, product shots, sound design, and motion graphics are used to make the message easier to understand and easier to buy from — every element earns its place.
3
Variants your media buyer can actually test
Instead of one beautiful video, you can get multiple angles, hooks, cutdowns, and formats — ready to drop into your ad account and run against each other immediately.

FAQ

Questions buyers
actually ask.

Do you guarantee results or ROAS? +
No. ROAS depends on your offer, targeting, landing page, budget, and traffic — none of which I control. I guarantee a polished, well-structured creative that gives the ad the best possible chance to perform.
What do you need from me to start? +
Product footage or visuals, your offer details, target audience, platform, and any references you like. The more context you give upfront, the faster and better the output.
How long does a video ad take? +
A single ad typically takes 2–4 days. An ad pack with multiple hooks and formats usually takes 4–7 days. Rush delivery is available — mention it in your brief.
Do you write the script too? +
Yes. Script, hook structure, and visual direction are all part of my process. You can also provide your own script — I'll work with whatever you have and improve it if needed.
Can you handle AI-generated visuals? +
Yes. I work with AI-generated footage from tools like Kling AI, Runway, and others — combining them with professional editing, sound, and motion to produce a polished finished creative.
Do you work with agencies? +
Yes. I regularly work as a white-label creative partner for digital marketing agencies. I match your brief format and deliver client-ready work with no branding from my end.
How many revisions do I get? +
As many as it takes to get it right. I don't count rounds and I don't charge extra for revisions. We stop when you're genuinely happy with the creative and ready to run it.
What platforms do you deliver for? +
Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Snapchat, and any other platform. I deliver all required aspect ratios (9:16, 16:9, 1:1, 4:5) in the correct specs for each platform.
